Our Story

Built on registration.
Grown through craft.

Amit Graphics started as a small print shop with one belief: nothing leaves the studio unless it's aligned, accurate, and ready for the real world.

Mission

To give every brand — from a first-time founder to an established enterprise — production quality that matches its ambition.

Vision

A studio where print craft and digital design are never separate disciplines — one brand, one register, every surface.

Values

Precision over speed. Honesty in proofing. Long-term partnerships over one-off jobs.

The Founder

A craftsman's approach to a digital age.

"A misaligned plate ruins a print run. A misaligned brand ruins a business. We treat both the same way — check twice, ship once."

Amit Graphics was founded on the print floor, not in a boardroom. That hands-on discipline — checking every plate, every proof, every pixel — still shapes how the studio operates today, whether the deliverable is a business card or a full digital platform.

Plate 04 — Timeline

How the studio grew.

Year 01

The Print Floor

Year 04

Design Studio Launch

Branding and identity design added as a core service, moving from execution to full creative direction.

Year 08

Digital Expansion

Web and digital campaign work introduced, bringing print-grade precision to on-screen brand experiences.

Today

Full-Service Studio

Amit Graphics now delivers brand strategy, print production, and digital design as one connected process.
